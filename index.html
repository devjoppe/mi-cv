Hi, my name is Joakim ...

... and I am a creative Frontend developer in training. I have years of experience working with UX/UI and design. With my new knowledge, my goal is to create solutions that are fun, useful and brings something new to the table (or screen in this case).

What I do? ❙

Code

I want to specialize in coding UIs and functionality that appeals to and helps the user to solve their tasks. As a new developer, I want to expand the knowledge needed to build solutions for the future. Currently, I focus on JavaScript and the React framework. But with time I look forward to digging deeper into alternative JavaScript frameworks (looking at you Vue).

Design

The role as a Frontend developer appealed to me as you have your hands in both jars (think this is a Swedish expression). Or, working closely with team members who have thier hand in the design jar. In my previous roles, I have worked with creating various types of interfaces and designs for different types of content. I have no problem working with tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma and Blender (for 3D visualization).
