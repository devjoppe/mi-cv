What I do

Code

Specialize in coding UIs and functionality that appeals and helps the user to solve their tasks. As a new developer, I want to expand the knowledge needed to build solutions for the future. Currently, I focus on JavaScript, TypeScript and the React framework. But with time I look forward to digging deeper into alternative JavaScript frameworks (looking at you Vue).

Design

The role as a Frontend developer appealed to me as you have your hands in both jars (think this is a Swedish expression). Or, working closely with team members who have thier hand in the design jar. In my previous roles, I have worked with creating various types of UIs and designs for different types of content. I have no problem working with tools such as Photoshop, Illustrator, XD, Figma and Blender (for 3D visualization).
